Corn Syrup

Corn syrup is a viscous, sweet syrup produced from the naturally occurring sugars in corn. It is known as Glucose Syrup and is a common ingredient in many popular desserts and beverages.

    Benefits and Uses
  • It provides sweetness intensity equivalent to sugar so can be substitute in many applications. Corn syrup provides soft, moist texture allowing production of chewy cookies, snack bars, and other baked goods.

  • It improves the flavour of soft drinks, fruit juices, yogurts, and other snacks. Enhances many fruit, citrus and spice flavours in beverages, bakery fillings, and dairy products.

  • It preserves and protects food by reducing water activity in products such as jams and jellies. Also, preserves texture of canned fruits and reduces freezer burn in frozen fruits.

  • Imparts browning and flavour to baked goods, including breads
