Corn Syrup

Corn syrup is a viscous, sweet syrup produced from the naturally occurring sugars in corn. It is known as Glucose Syrup and is a common ingredient in many popular desserts and beverages.

    Benefits and Uses
  • Provides a sustained source of energy as it is rich in carbohydrates and calories
  • Gluten-free and allergen-friendly
  • It provides a quick and sustained source of energy, making it an excellent choice for those who lead active lifestyles
  • Tapioca syrup is low in fat and protein. Also, contains essential minerals like potassium and calcium
  • It can replace sugar in recipes, add sweetness to beverages and desserts, and even be incorporated into sauces and dressings.
  • Its mild flavour blends well with other ingredients and provides a subtle sweetness that’s not overpowering.
  • It has a lower glycaemic index compared to corn syrup or regular sugar, which means it won’t cause sharp spikes in blood sugar levels.
